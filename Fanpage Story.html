The Story Of Half Life

Half-Life 1

You play as Gordon Freeman, a 27-Year old man working at the top secret underground research facility, Black Mesa, located in the middle of the New Mexico desert. You arrive to work one day 5 minutes late and you were meant to do an experiment which would blast a laser into a alien ore for research purposes. However, they decide to create 2 crucial mistakes. First, the scientists use a much more powerful sample of ore and they also increased the AMS Beam power by 105%. As you step into the test chamber, you must insert the ore into the AMS beam. This opens a portal from Earth to an alien planet called Xen. This causes the creatures from Xen to infiltrate the Earth and trying to take over the earth. Your goal is to reach the surface and alert the government that you are stranded and the facility is in ruins. However, that plan quickly backfires when the US military infiltrate the research facility and kill all the witnesses in attempt to cover up the entire project. You now have many weapons to fight the Xen creatures and the US military and your new goal is to get help via radio signals to the surface. You work your way through many chapters and areas of the Black Mesa Testing Facility, until you reach the Lambda Complex which is an area of the building used to open portals. You then jump through a portal one of the scientists made to go to Xen. Your goal in Xen is to kill the Nihilanth which is stopping the radio signals from being sent to the surface in earth. To first get past the Nihilanth, you need to get past the gonarch which is a giant monster that is trying to kill you, but after you get past it, you then need to kill the Nihilanth, which looks like a giant baby. After you kill it, a mysterious figure appears named the G-Man who offers you a deal to work with him. If you accept, he will make you step through a portal and the game will end. However, If you don't accept it, he will teleport you into a room full of alien grunts who will kill you and then the game will also end.

Half-Life 2

You wake up 20 years after the Black Mesa incident still as Gordon Freeman and the G-Man puts you on a train into City-17. You realise that a powerful, alien empire called The Combine have taken over the earth within just 7 hours. You quickly find your old colleagues and friends from Black Mesa and Your goal is to escape from the city. You manage to make your way to your old friends lab, Dr Kleiner's, and meet your friend Eli and realise he has a daughter named Alyx. Your new goal is to get to the Citadel controlling everything and take it down. you also realise your old boss, Dr Breen, had surrendered the Earth to the Combine purely to make his life the best and make everybody else suffer. You try to use a teleporter to get to Eli's lab but it goes horribly wrong and you have to manually make your way there. You eventually get there and recieve the gravity gun which can pick up anything. Next, you make your way to the Citadel while gathering a huge army of people following you and eventually getting inside, killing Dr Breen and destroying the core of the building, then an explosion occurs and the G-Man talks to you, saying you have done well but this is not over yet. Getting to this stage is a challenge and there are many boss fights and obstacles in the way. It is one of the greatest videogames of all time and has won over 50 awards.

Half-Life 2: Episode 1

You wake up straight after you collapse the Citadel. You have been knocked to the floor and Alyx finds you. You need to contain the Citadel core to make sure everybody can safetly evacuate City 17. You eventually contain the core, then you make your way to the centre of the city. You find many survivors and your new goal is to get yourself, Alyx, Barney and some survivors to evacuate City-17. You defeat many combine soldiers and eventually take a train out of the city, However, the Citadel reactor core explodes and the train got destroyed.

Half-Life 2: Episode 2

You escape the train and manage to find Alyx and you find a building containing a drive with necessary information for the combine portal to close. To make it work you need to make it to White Forest, a camp which is home to Dr Kleiner and Eli and is being managed by Dr Magnusson. However, on the way, Alyx gets killed by a Combine Hunter. You find a cave home to many survivors but it is also home to many antlions and they are being under attack. You help fight them and you eventually win. The vortigaunt's end up reviving Alyx with a magical crystal and you both make a long, difficult drive to White Forest encountering many obstacles in the way. When you get their, you survive 2 HUGE battles and you shoot a rocket into the combine portal. You then try to escape the facility but Eli is killed and the game ends.
